Mat Hayward

Mat Hayward started the Rocktographer group with an event in 2012 as a way to shine the spotlight on the incredibly talented group of photographers in the Seattle area. Getty Images , 107.7 The End , and Seattle Music Insider. Mat has been passionate about music his entire life but sadly was born with virtually no rythym. As a result, he turned to his other passion...photography. Mat's work has been published in many major media outlets including an image featured by Rolling Stone Magazine as one of the Top 40 concert photos of 2013 .

Mat has always been a rocker at heart with his favorite bands being Jane's Addiction and Nine Inch Nails.
