Bisagra

Based in Peru. Active since 2014

Bisagra is a project by Andrés Pereira Paz, Christian Luza, Eliana Otta, Florencia Portocarrero, Iosu Aramburu, Kenji Quispe, Maria José Murillo and Miguel A. López.

Bisagra functions as a meeting point in the fragmented world of the visual arts in Lima and the region, creating connections among its different manifestations, as well as other areas of research and creation. Heir to previous experiences of some of its members’ collective work (such as La Culpable, La Casa Rosa, and Charla Parásita ), Bisagra seeks to function as a space capable of embracing and circulating ideas, projects and experiments that share an interest in flexible exchanges with a critical and humorous spirit.
