Rainchasers

A collection of kayaking or canoeing whitewater adventures in the UK to browse, search, and favourite in the hope of weekend rain. Gauge levels calibrated to the specific runs are included to allow you to judge the likely paddling conditions.

Currently Includes...

  • Cilau Aeron to Aberaeron (Aeron)
  • Llangeitho to Ciliau Aeron (Aeron)
  • Blaengwynfi/Cymmer to Pontrhydyfen (Afan)
  • Pontrhydyfen to Aberavon (Afan)
