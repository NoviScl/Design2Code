International Students

WSU has close to 3,000 international students and scholars from across the globe. Our international students and scholars are part of the WSU Warrior family! The Office of International Students and Scholars (OISS)  at Wayne State University are here to support and enhance your educational, cultural and social experiences. OISS collaborates with colleges, departments and the community to create and maintain an inclusive, global campus.
