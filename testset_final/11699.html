4th of July Luminaries

Light up your space with these patriotic 4th of July luminaries. Perfect for Memorial Day, Independence Day, Patriot's Day or for all year round!
Author: Amanda Formaro

Supplies

  • Glass jars
  • Red white and blue acrylic paint
  • White piece of paper
  • Black Sharpie

Instructions

  • Draw a star on a piece of paper. Place the drawing underneath a piece of Contact paper.
  • Cut the star out and peel off the backing. Place the star onto the jar and press to make sure that there are no air bubbles and that all corners are pressed down.
  • Paint jar with one coat of acrylic paint and let dry completely.
  • Make sure the paint is dry before peeling the contact paper off. Pipe glitter glue around the star and let dry several hours.
