Our Team
Peter M. Thomas
President, Long View Capital

Peter M. Thomas currently serves as the President of Long View Capital, LLC. Long View Capital oversees real estate, debt, equity, and private equity investments. He has been managing a private family investment office since 2002. Prior to that, Thomas was an Investment Advisor for Smith Barney where he worked for four years. Before joining Smith Barney, Thomas was an Account Executive for CBS Television in its national sales division.
