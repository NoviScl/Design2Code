Play as Mask
Win all gold belts with all regular fighters except one in legend mode to unlock Mask.

Play as Skyscrape
Win all gold belts with all regular fighters in legend mode to unlock Skyscrape.

Play as Larry Landless
Win the silver belt with all characters except one in championship mode to unlock Larry Landless.

Play as Big John McCarthy
Win the silver belt with all characters in championship mode to unlock Big John McCarthy.

Mask:
Enter arcade mode. When the best record appears, press Right, Left, Right, Left, X + Y. Mask should be now be unlocked. Alternately, clear legend mode with all hidden characters.

Big John McCarthy:
Unlock all regular fighters, then win all Silver belts in championship mode with all of them.

Bruce Buffer:
Win 33 consecutive matches in arcade mode.

Frank Fertitta:
Lose 66 bouts in arcade mode.

Larry Landless:
Unlock all regular fighters, then win all Silver belt in championship mode with all but one of them.

Skyscrape:
Clear legend mode with all normal characters.

Additional fighters:
Win five consecutive matches to unlock the following fighters, in order. The next fighter in line will be unlocked with your next streak of five wins.
