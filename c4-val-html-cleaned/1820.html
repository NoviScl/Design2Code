Welcome

Welcome to this special ship management edition of ITIC’s electronic newsletter, The Wire, specifically for ship managers. ITIC is the world’s largest liability insurer of ship, crew, commercial, pool and yacht managers with over 400 companies insured for these activities. Ship managers account for over 20% of ITIC’S total membership.

ITIC contributes a column in Ship Management International magazine, which covers a variety of topics from mediation, regulatory issues, regional jurisdiction issues and cyber crime. This column is written by various members of the ITIC team.

In this edition of The Wire we have compiled  and curated a selection of our recent articles, however you can read all of these columns through the Ship Management International website.

We hope you will find this collection of articles of interest.
