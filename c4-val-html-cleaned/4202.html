James S. Miller and Andrew V. Z. Brower

Containing group: Dioptini

Introduction

The species listed here are dioptines of uncertain affinity, either because their type specimens are in poor condition and lack diagnostic characters, or because they exhibit features that do not indicate unambiguous affinity with any recognized genus. They await additional study (Miller, 2009).
