• Taste port and wine at 5 wine cellars
  • Visit the cultural highlights - museum, DR Church, 27 wall hangings in the church hall, Arts Meander, freestyle organ recitals
  • Browse through the collection of second hand books and Africana at the museum
  • Bring your mountain bike or running shoes and get fit.  We have lots of fresh air and safe roads.
  • Try our local cuisine - Karoo lamb, bobotie or ostrich specialities
  • Go fishing or have a picnic at the Nelsrivier Dam
  • Buy fruit - fresh, dried or jarred!
  • Take a siesta like the locals do in summer and soak up the winter sun
  • Take a stroll through the Hennie Cloete Nature Garden to see why we are famous for succulents
  • Soak up the mineral waters of the Spa (25km)
  • Watch the birds - ostriches and the smaller ones
  • Buy a handmade pipe or knife at Ebenhardts and send a friend a local postcard
  • Visit Gamkaberg Nature Reserve - Home of the endangered mountain zebra. 
  • Pick your own grapes at De Krans during February or apricots during November.
  • Enjoy a meal at one of the local restaurants – there are at least 4 restaurants within 1 km from the guest house.
