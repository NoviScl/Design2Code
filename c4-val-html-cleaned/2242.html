VVB LAUFERSWEILER TED – Technical Editors Department


Offers technical editing of scientific, engineering, medical, or other complex documents for both language and content issues not only in the German language. The language aspect covers traditional copyediting and production editing concerns; the content aspect involves substantive editing to address the accuracy and completeness of the technical information and to ensure that it is intelligible to your intended audience.

Our Technical Editor Department reviews documentation prepared to explain complex technical topics or concepts like manuals or guides, software guidance and hardware products, or information catering to a niche audience. A technical editor edits the documentation for completeness and accuracy, unity and integrity, language errors, and for the right level of complexity for the users.
