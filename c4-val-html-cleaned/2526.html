Location:   97 Grafton Road is a glass office building located opposite the Auckland Hospital car park. It shares a driveway with 93 Grafton Road.
Please note: Grafton Specialists is situated on the ground floor. Allergies New Zealand, located on the basement level, is not part of Grafton Specialists.
Motorways:  

From the North Shore, after the harbour bridge, take the North Western Motorway East (SH16) towards the "Port" Exit 426B (not Helensville!). Stay in the very left lane. This exit is directly after Cook St. Then take the Wellesley St exit.

From the Southern Motorway, after passing the Khyber Pass Exit, stay in the left lane and follow the motorway signs to "Port"(SH16). Then take the Wellesley St exit.

From the North Western Motorway, follow the signs to the "Port" (SH16) This is after the Newton Road exit. Stay in the right lane! Then take the Wellesley St exit.

Turn right at the first lights into Wellesley Street East (towards Grafton, Auckland Hospital).
Then go straight through the next lights into Grafton Road. 97 Grafton Road is on the right. It shares a driveway with 93 Grafton Road.
Parking:  

Limited patient parking is available down the driveway on the left hand side.
The first 8 car parks on the left have blue signs with "Grafton Specialists Parking" to indicate patient parking. Parking is only for the duration of your consultation. You will be required to register your car at reception.
Paid parking is generally available if these spaces are full.
Due to traffic congestion on the motorways and city, please allow sufficient time for travel and parking before your appointment!!
Public Transport:  

Bus stops for the Link buses and other main routes are located near Auckland Hospital, on Park Road, which is 2-3 minutes walk. Grafton Train Station is approximately a 15 minute walk.
Contact 09 366 6400 for timetable information.
