New Roof

The Children’s Home recently got a New Roof! No longer will the Home Mothers have to run for buckets and towels to catch the water that is dripping inside, or move the beds so the children don’t get wet!

This roof has been a long time coming and everyone at the Home are grateful to those who pledged and participated at the 4th Annual Fun Run-Walk in October 2013, the only major fund-raiser for the Home.
