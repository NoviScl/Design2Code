Research Notes

A couple of possibles christenings for William
(1) 11 Aug 1611 Saint Martin On Looe Bay, Cornwall, 30 miles East from Saint Just In Roseland.
(2) 15 Feb 1626 East Newlyn, Cornwall, 12 miles North  from Saint Just In Roseland.
A possible christening for Ann
22 Mar 1617 Marhamchurch, Cornwall, - unlikely, it is fifty miles away on the north coast
    At this stage, declare the trail run dry for both

Response from Graham Warmington, OPC St Gluvias, Penryn
     I found the reference, you mentioned, to William & Ann wedding in 1646. Unfortunately this is the only Andrew/Androus wedding in the parish during the 17th Century. However, I have found reference to a few other Lamertons who were married after Ann. I can happily send dates/names for these.
    The Parish christening register only goes back to 1727, and there are no relevant grave stones in the cemetery.
    I had a quick look at the wills website: http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/documentsonline/wills.asp but couldn't really find anything relevant. Maybe you could take a more thorough look.
