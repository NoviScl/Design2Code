↑ About Ridgways

Ridgways , sometimes called Ridgways of London , is a UK-based tea company, founded in 1836 as a small tea shop in London, by Thomas Ridgway.

Ridgways teas are sold in tea bags and are widely available in the UK, and can be purchased online in the US through a variety of retailers.
