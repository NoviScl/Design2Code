About Us

Costello Design has designed and developed more than 100 products that have been manufactured and placed into the marketplace. Costello Design was founded in 1996 by James J. Costello, an award winning Industrial Designer, with more than 20 years experience in consumer product design. He has received awards from the Industrial Designers Society of America, Business Week Magazine, DuPont and Appliance Manufacturer Magazine. As a graduate of the prestigious Domus Academy in Milan, Italy, Mr. Costello's international design view is evident in the products he has created for the marketplace. Mr. Costello was the one-man Industrial Design Department for Igloo Products Corp. His contribution to Igloo was in transforming their product line from the ordinary to a distinctive brand identity.

At Costello Design we have generated work for our clients in areas of housewares, lighting, furniture, personal care products, insulated products, home electronics, outdoor products, plastic products, and appliances. We offer full service Industrial Design & Product Development Services. Our business is to provide our clients with innovative product designs. We believe successful products satisfy your marketing, manufacturing and budget goals and, at the same time, will give the consumer a well-designed, attractive, user-friendly product. Our creative design solutions are a fusion of marketing, aesthetics, and ergonomics, function and manufacturing.
