About Us

Welcome to the Northern California Miniature  Horse Club also known as NCMHC. We are the second oldest sanctioned  Club in the American Miniature Horse Association which was incorporated  in July of 1978.


The  NCMHC is recognized as a quality show Club managing three to four shows  per year. From Halter to performance hunting, jumping, and driving the  NCMHC members are known for their quality horses.


The AMHA  miniature horse is truly the horse for everyone, the average full grown  horse weights 200 to 250 pounds and grows to a maximum of 34 inches at  the last mane hair. It can be handled by children as well as adults of  all ages.


The miniature horse eats  about 1/5 that of a full size horse and takes much less room. They are  great as pets, lawn mowers, and wonderful to have around.


So come on out and join us, have some fun, and experience the great American Miniature Horse.
