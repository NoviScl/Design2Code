GATHERED BY GRACE
God’s call to follow is an invitation to freedom. This is freedom to revel in the Spirit’s fruits: love, joy, peace, patience.

Discuss in your household or small group: 
* What do you think Paul means by the “yoke of slavery”? 
* How does Paul define “freedom” in verse 13? 
* How would you express the message of verses 16-21 in your own words? How does verse 18 express Paul’s meaning for “freedom”?
* Paul says that we are to live by the Spirit and its fruit. For which spiritual fruit do you most often pray?
* What would daily life be like if we were to allow such fruit to guide us?

FOR FURTHER REFLECTION  

Devotional Practice: 

"Foxes have holes, and birds of the air have nests; but the Son of Man has nowhere to lay his head."  In your devotion times this week, think of the people around the world who do not have homes where they may live. Some homes have been lost by disaster or conflict. Some folks cannot afford shelter. Include these people in your prayers throughout the week.

Summer Ritual: 
Summer is a glorious time to celebrate the goodness of God and the sacredness of life. Take some quiet time alone each week this summer.
