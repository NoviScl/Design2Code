company history

 

I have always enjoyed building and fixing things. This enthusiasm lead to an interest in  creating high quality wood furniture and other products. After a year or so, many customers were curious if I was able to do home repairs. Over time, the woodworking was phased out and I began to do handyman work and home improvements for customers full time. Through many years of doing this type of work, I am happy to say I chose the right pathway, as I am always excited to meet and interact with new individuals every day.

 

I am the sole owner and operator of TCDwoodworks. I have had a passion for working on homes and being hands-on for all of my life. For many years, I have been happily serving the homeowners of the Frederick, MD region. I am also a candidate for graduation at Frostburg State University, for a degree in Small Business/ Entrepreneurship.

 

Our mission is to provide the highest quality design, renovation and construction services available, while maintaining a level of customer satisfaction matched by no one.


We are happy to provide multiple customer references for any job upon request. Our customers are very happy to share their experiences with other homeowners interested in working with us on their home renovation projects. Word of mouth is our BEST advertising!
