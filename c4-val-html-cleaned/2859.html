The Long Hall Pub & Grocery is a traditional Irish pub located in the heart of Midtown Manhattan on 34th Street. With a selection of domestic beer; international craft beers on tap (including the perfect pint of Guinness!) and dozens more by the bottle, New York City's beer seekers will always feel right at home at The Long Hall. The Long Hall is the perfect venue for your next private party or corporate event. From small gatherings, birthday parties & holidays to private corporate events, our private 2nd floor space can handle parties and events of all sizes. Come in and watch the G.A.A. & rugby games and avail of our bottomless brunch, lunch specials or dinner menus The next time you want a genuine taste of Ireland, look no further than The Long Hall in New York City!
