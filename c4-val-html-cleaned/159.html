Our regular ZeroMQ Meetup in London, at Skills Matter .

In this session, we'll build a distributed chat application that does discovery and implements a multithreaded UI in 50 lines of (C) code.

You should come with your laptop and ZeroMQ installed. Ideally, libzmq and CZMQ, but any language will do (your mileage will vary).

Where: Skills Matter, 116-120 Goswell Rd, London, EC1V 7DP, United Kingdom

When: 16 April, 6.30pm

Attendees Please do add your name to ensure a seat.

  1. Ashic Mahtab (organiser)
  2. Pieter Hintjens (presenter)
