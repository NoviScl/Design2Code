About Us

Siro Derrer-Brooks has an incredible eye for fashion and decor, and has worked in the industry for many years. With experience as a personal shopper and wardrobe designer at high end stores like Nordstrom and Jacobon's, Siro was able to open her own boutique. Her eyes were on a prime business location, the retail space right across from the famous Friendly Tavern. A lot of remodeling and renovating had to be done in order to get the gorgeous boutique to the place it is now; however, it took a lot longer than planned. Derrer-Brooks had to open a boutique on a street just off of Main Street in order to establish herself as a boutique that knows how to sell. After months on Cedar, November 2014 came and Siro was able to open in her current location! With help from family and friends, she built each of the racks that hold the exquisite garments, as well as the two dressing rooms. The boutique stands proud with the rest of the Main Street businesses, as it is here to stay.
