Sir Terry Pratchett R.I.P.

We are just hearing the breaking news of the death of our Special Guest, Sir Terry Pratchett. I am sure everyone who was at the opening ceremony of Satellite 4 will remember the kind words he sent to us. It was a great pity his health, even at that time, was too poor to allow him to join us. He was missed then and he will be missed even more now. Satellite 4 would like to send its deepest condolences to his family and friends at this time.

Satellite 4 Souvenir Book

We will very soon be posting out our Satellite 4 Souvenir Book to all members. The book contains articles and photos of the convention, including contributions from all our Guests of Honour. In addition, we are also including a limited edition Jim Burns print based on the programme book cover. This will only be available for Satellite 4 members and we would like to give it to you as a 'Thank You' for supporting our convention.

In order to receive your Souvenir Book and limited edition print, we need to make sure we have your correct postal address. If you have changed address since you registered for Satellite 4, can you please update your details through the convention website by following the instructions on the My Membership page, or email us at membership@satellite4.org.uk with the updated address. We must receive these details by Friday 21st November at the latest to guarantee correct delivery.

Finally, if you are attending Novacon, please pick up your copy of the Souvenir Book and Jim Burns print from the Satellite convention desk in the Dealers' Room.
