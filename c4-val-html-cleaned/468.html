Manchester, Salford and Greater Manchester

This great Victorian city of the industrial revolution, Manchester has grown from a market town to become the trading heart of Britain. It is now at the centre of the north of England and becoming one of the UK's most iconic cities.

Manchester and the surrounding region is undergoing many changes. Hotels are springing up in old abandoned warehouses, office blocks and new developments such as the MediaCityUK in Salford Quays and the Trafford Centre are creating new leisure destinations.
