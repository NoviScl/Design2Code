The tenth century Armenian Church of the Holy Cross or Surp Khach on the island of Aghtamar is one of the most important architectural sites in Turkey. With its unique sculptural carvings depicting a complex of biblical and secular themes, this church is a jewel of medieval Armenain and early Christian art and architecture.
