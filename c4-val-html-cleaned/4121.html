The Story

As a classic car enthusiast, and with experience within the motor trade & Motorsports, I began restoring and painting Reliant Scimitars as a hobby. With reasonably priced paint shops willing to tackle fibreglass cars thin on the ground, I was soon asked if I would paint for other owners.

It's not only Fibreglass cars though, as have worked on a wide range of more conventional bodied cars, I've even let the odd modern car sneak into the workshop !

Please take a look at the gallery, where some of the cars are featured and don't hesitate to get in touch with any questions you may have.
