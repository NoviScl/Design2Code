August 27-September 2, 2012

Report on Contracts Signed
NYC Residential Properties
$4 Million and Above

9 Contracts Signed

I am here to report that we are closing out the long, hot summer of 2012 with 9 contracts signed last week, streaking to a record 129 contracts for the 10-week period that began on June 25 and ended on the day before Labor Day. This is a 42% increase over last summer and the best summer since we started keeping score in 2007 (see box score below).

The No.1 contract last week was the Penthouse Triplex at 482 Greenwich Street, asking $11,500,000, in a new condo building that should be completed by the end of the year. The 4,675 square foot unit has 4 bedrooms, 6.5 baths, and a 22’ ceiling in the living room that opens to a fabulous wraparound terrace.

Speaking of new construction, there are going to be quite a few new condo projects slated to come on the market in the next 6 months that will be sold off of floorplans and renderings. Most of these buildings will not be completed until 2014 and beyond.
