Seducer's Embrace - Live in Moscow, Russia, 2003
 Venue: 'Total Fucking Armageddon!' Fest, 'Relax' Club: Moscow, Russia
 Date: November 29, 2003
 Time: 27:20
 Size: 1 CD
 Sound: B+/A-, Master
 Notes: 'Total Fucking Armageddon' was a fest with some of the best Russian underground acts. The bands represented various styles of music – from Swedish melodic death metal to true black. Half of the bands already well-known to the audience and opened for foreign bands who played here in Russia. Here you can find some pics from the fest and gig report (Russian only).
Seducer's Embrace are one of the most well-known bands here. They perform very nice melodic death metal. Some songs presented @ the gig were from the upcoming CD.
The other bands played at the fest were: Inexist , Horned Necrocannibals , Crystal Abyss , Morrah , and the gig crowned by a great Cover’s Jam Session .
