Releasing . . . again

After teaching how to let go how to release the ties that bind one to the no longer necessary connections, I still feel that releasing is not the easiest task at hand.

I remember too much. I think of the shorthand we used: IMU, ILU, IWU, M2.

And all that. Just meditate. That's the remedy. Maybe not the cure, but it's the remedy.
