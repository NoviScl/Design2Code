The sixth word of verse (19:10) is a perfect verb (فعل ماض). The verb is third person masculine singular. The verb's triliteral root is qāf wāw lām (ق و ل).

Chapter (19) sūrat maryam (Mary)


(19:10:6)
qāla
He said,
V – 3rd person masculine singular perfect verb فعل ماض

Verse (19:10)

The analysis above refers to the tenth verse of chapter 19 (sūrat maryam):

Sahih International: [Zechariah] said, "My Lord, make for me a sign." He said, "Your sign is that you will not speak to the people for three nights, [being] sound."

Quran Recitation by Saad Al-Ghamadi
