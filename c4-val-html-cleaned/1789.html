Guy Mitchell
Styles: Pop
Catalogue Number: ADDCD3134
Guy Mitchell was one of the few artists whose career spanned both the traditional middle-of-the-road pop era of the early ‘50s and the rock ‘n’ roll explosion which dominated the latter half of the decade. He was equally popular on both sides of the Atlantic, enjoying a string of hits during the early ‘50s in the USA before the UK gave him a run of success when things went quiet in the US, followed by his dramatic return to the top of the charts with the landmark international success of “Singing The Blues”, and other significant follow-up successes, including his final No.
