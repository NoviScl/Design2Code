What is Garcinia Cambogia?

Garcinia Cambogia is the small, sweet tasting green and yellow fruit that closely resembles a small pumpkin. These fruits are the product of the native Indonesian Garcinia gummi-gutta tree.

What are the uses for Garcinia Cambogia?

The main use of Garcinia Cambogia is as a food source. In Asia, it has become a common cooking ingredient for many dishes, especially curries.

What is a Garcinia Cambogia Diet?

This diet involves consuming Garcinia Cambogia supplements daily in order to gain results in both suppressing appetite and stopping the build-up of bodily fat. This is due to the high levels of HCA found within the fruit which reduce the conversion of carbohydrates into fat.
