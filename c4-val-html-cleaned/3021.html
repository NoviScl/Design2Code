Charitable Lead Trust

Protect Your Assets

If you want to make an impact now at Lake Highland Preparatory School and also provide for your family later, consider setting up a charitable lead trust. You transfer cash or other assets to a trust that makes payments to Lake Highland Preparatory School for a period of time.

There are two ways that charitable lead trusts make payments to LHP:

A charitable lead annuity trust pays a fixed amount each year to LHP and is more attractive when interest rates are low.

A charitable lead unitrust pays a variable amount each year to LHP based on the value of the assets in the trust. With a unitrust, if the trust's assets go up in value, for example, the payments to LHP go up as well.
