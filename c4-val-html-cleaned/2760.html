Information about CSBK

Website: https://www.csbk.ca/

The Parts Canada Superbike Championship is the national-level racing body.

The championship is run over 7 rounds taking place at Shannoville (2 rounds), Mt Tremblant, Mosport (2 rounds), Race City (Calgary), and Atlantic Motorsports Park (Shubenacadie, NS).

To participate in National racing events you must possess a current Nationally sanctioned race license.
