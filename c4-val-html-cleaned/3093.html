Welcome to the Wooden Nickel

Inside the Wooden Nickel Bar

The Wooden Nickel, on historic Market Square Millersburg specializes in seafood, steaks, and pasta, as well as offering daily and weekend dinner specials.

The kitchen is open Wednesday through Saturday until 8 pm and Sunday until 6 pm, serving lunch and dinner.  Our menu features a wide variety of seafood dishes, flame-grilled steaks, pork chops, and chicken, home-made pasta and pizza, along with salads, appetizers and desserts.  The bar offers daily and weekend drink specials as well as a complete variety of cold take-out beer.  The bar has eight flat screen televisions, so patrons can enjoy sports programming all year long.

The cozy atmosphere features colors of hunter green and peach with accents of polished brass and finished oak wooden tables.  The property also has an adjacent 80 seat banquet facility for private parties and functions, and  a separate game room featuring a regulation size pool table, video games, pinball machines, and bar height tables with stools.

Gift cards can be purchased in any amount and are redeemed much the same as a credit card.

Reservations are not necessary, but are accepted by calling 717-692-3003.
