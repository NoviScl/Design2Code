Which Crust?

Original Thin Crust Our famous thin crust made from Aldo's original recipe. Once you try this crispy, flaky crust you'll fall in love.

Whole Wheat Counting carbs? This healthy version of our original thin crust has the complex carbs that modern diets recommend without sacrificing taste.

Thick Crust Hearty appetites will love our deep-dish crust, smothered with our homemade spicy sauce. ($1 extra)

California Crust A unique west coast creation - lighter bready crust, not too thick, not too thin. ($1 extra)

Petite and Giant available only with California crust.
