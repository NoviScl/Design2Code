Free online lessons

If you are a distance learner, these may work well for you...

  • LearnDutch.org  - Online course for learning the Dutch language.
  • Taalthuis.com  - Free online Dutch lessons, including video lessons.
  • FreeDutch.nl - free Dutch lessons as well as a blog about the Dutch language and Holland.
  • DutchGrammar.com  - free lessons covering various parts of speech.
