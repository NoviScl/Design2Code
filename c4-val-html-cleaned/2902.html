As design and managing principal, Susan Marcotullio is responsible for principal and senior level leadership including team and project management, programming, feasibility planning, design direction, quality assurance, construction document and construction administration covering the client's needs on all levels with attentive leadership.

During her years of experience she has directed the space analysis and successful build out of large corporate and financial offices, trading rooms, post production facilities, healthcare, schools, tenant fit-outs, data centers, retail stores, newspaper production facilities, fast track and specialty design projects.

Susan's in-depth knowledge in the execution of all aspects of project planning and implementation, including design, construction, furniture, ergonomic planning and project management, is appealing to clients. She is considered a trusted advisor and has a following of repeat clients that she has serviced for many years. Her leadership and attention to detail leads the path to successful projects.

Recent accomplishments include the relocation of sizeable financial institutions and bank headquarters, relocation of various TV show entertainment offices and post-production facilities, and the design of NYC flagship retail stores for leading corporations. She is a graduate of Michigan State University and was an associate at a New York architectural firm before establishing Frank & Marcotullio Design.

Her previous experience includes the planning, design and management of interior construction of corporate offices, nursing homes, a hospice, housing developments for the elderly and handicapped, adult day care centers, and prominent national retail shops such as Laura Ashley and The Forgotten Woman.

Susan is a LEED ID+C Accredited Professional and practices sustainability on all projects that she leads.
