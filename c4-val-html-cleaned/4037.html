2023 - 2024 Information and Service Logs 
The TEAL CARES Program encourages all Aliso Niguel High School students to spread the Teal Wolverine Spirit throughout our community by volunteering 25 hours to charitable organizations or events of their choice.  When students care about their community, they learn the joy of giving back and a compassion for helping others which lasts a lifetime. 

Let’s show our community that Teal Cares!

Once a student has completed and turned in their volunteer hours log, they will receive a certificate from the ANHS PTSA President and ANHS Principal in recognition of their generous support to our community.

Steps to complete the TEAL CARES Program:

  1.   Complete the Teal Cares Volunteer Hours Log with the appropriate signatures each time you volunteer.    Volunteer hours should be unpaid hours helping a non-profit organization.  
    Ideas include, but not limited to: volunteering at: Libraries, Animal Shelters, City Events, Non-Profit Fundraisers, After School Programs for Kids, Tutoring Programs, Religious Organizations, Environmental Clean-Ups, etc.

  2. Once you have volunteered for at least 25 hours of community service, turn in your Teal Cares Volunteer Hours Log to the ANHS PTSA Mailbox.  All forms due no later than TBD, 2024 .    Note: Although students are encouraged to complete additional volunteer hours, only 25 hours are required to complete the Teal Cares Program.  No additional hours need to be recorded or documented.

  3. Students will be invited to receive a certificate from the ANHS Principal & PTSA President. (Certificates will be distributed throughout the year)

ANHS and ANHS PTSA do not endorse any particular charities or community service events.
Parents and students must do their own due diligence before working with any organization.
Any community service activities are done at the student’s own risk/liability, and must be done with their parent’s permission.
Volunteer hours must be completed 6/8/22 through 4/15/24 to qualify for the 2023-24 Teal Cares Program. 
School Service Hours performed during Detention (etc) do not count towards Teal Cares Service Hours.
