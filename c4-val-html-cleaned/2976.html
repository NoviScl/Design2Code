Westward Ho!


Full of surprises this Victorian Seaside resort not only holds many memories but it continues to provide a sense of romance, mystery and intrigue. The long golden beach, flanked by its unique pebble ridge edges onto Northam Burrows with its long grasses and sandy Dunes and is credited with being part of a designated site of Scientific interest and also forms part of the United Nations Biosphere Reserve.

Westward Ho! is known for the roaring sea crashing against the pebbleridge and the sound of the pebbles rolling backwards and forwards in time to the sea. The amazing rock pools are full of sea life and the reflections in the sand are a true phenomena. Spectacular sunrises and sunsets adorn the skies and the majestic colours paint the horizon with awe and beauty.A lovely place to visit in 2023.
