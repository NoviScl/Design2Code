2007-05-30: Storm Warning
Roleplay Logs » Volume 01: Beginnings » 2007-05-30: Storm Warning

Starring:

Summary: During a break in training at the Kirby Plaza Building. Ryan meets Elle. Ryan also manages to not have Elle zap him

Date It Happened: May 30, 2007

Storm Warning

Midtown, NYC - Kirby Plaza Building

It's mid-morning on Friday, and the halls of the Kirby Plaza Building are filled with the usual, regulated activity. Part of the activity is Ryan Cochrane's sessions to try and gain more control over his powers.

Elle is heading down the hallway. She's been busy of late, trying to juggle a life with a Company job, and it has her a little exasperated.

Ryan cracks his neck a few times and then gets up. He sees the girl passing by and tries to get her attention.

Elle looks over as she hears the voice, and turns her head. She considers Ryan a moment, looking him up and down, before a neutral "Yes?"

Ryan smiles a little bit. "Umm…which way to the…restroom?"

Elle's voice is just a little biting. "Ooh, they're letting you go all by yourself?"

Ryan gives Elle a wry look. "Thanks.

Elle nods. "Not so big a fan of puppies messing on the floor.

Ryan chuckles some. "Yeah, I didn't say I had to go right now.

Elle takes a step over. "Elle Bishop."

Ryan shrugs, as if his powers weren't any big deal. "Weather control.

Elle can't help but grin at the last category. "So show me.

Ryan nods, "Sure…." He closes his eyes to concentrate and the area gets colder.

Elle grins. "Show me the lightning."

Ryan chuckles nervously. "Umm…yeah…that's not such a good idea.

The blonde grins at that. "Got it.

Ryan shrugs and dissipates the fog. "Actually, I'd rather only use my powers to counter anyone that's abusing weather controlling abilities, or to limit the damage caused by natural weather phenomenon.

Elle laughs amusedly at that. "Of course you would."

Ryan smirks. "And what is it that you can do?"

Elle holds up a hand, cupped, fingers up, and lets her electricity gather into the palm of her hand. "Lightning.

Ryan blinks and nods, suitably impressed. "Yeah, no wonder you think in terms of combat usefulness…."

Elle has a little half-smirk on her face. "I do what needs doing.

Ryan nods at Elle's words. "Okay.

Elle nods. "Good luck around here.

Ryan nods quietly to himself. "Yeah….I think I might."
