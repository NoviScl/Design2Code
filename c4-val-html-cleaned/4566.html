Varna: Nationalen Teatr

Country: Bulgaria
City: Varna
Location: ploshad Nezavisimost 1 (ploshad 9 Septemvri)

Built by Nikola Lazarov. Opened 1932. Home to the Drama Theatre "Stoyan Bachvarov" company and the "Derzhavna Opera" (state opera) company.
