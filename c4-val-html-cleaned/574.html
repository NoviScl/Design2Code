Health Education plays a crucial role in the development of a healthy environment and society. The journal disseminates good practice in health education to enable you to build on the best ideas of others. Offering stimulating and incisive coverage of current concerns and campaign strategies, it provides a wealth of practical information that can be used to enhance the effectiveness of your own health campaigns.
