France Lands Sixth EuroMillions Jackpot Win of 2013

A ticket purchased in France won Friday’s Euromillions jackpot worth €15,000,000 / £12,843,000, making it the sixth French EuroMillions jackpot win of 2013. Along with the jackpot winner, Friday’s draw saw 2,710,031 players also win prizes.

 

The Euromillions numbers that gave France their 66th Euromillions jackpot win were 01, 15, 28, 35 and 47 and Lucky Stars 01 and 07. Eight tickets matched five main numbers with one Lucky Star to each win the second-tier Euromillions prize worth €219,135 / £149,161 and 22 tickets won €26,561 / £18,080 each by matching five main numbers.

Following this, 67 players matched four numbers and both Lucky Stars to each win €4,360 / £2,968 and 1,139 players each won €224 / £152 by matching four numbers and one Lucky Star. In addition there was a UK player who won £1 million by matching Friday’s Millionaire Raffle number SXP713248.

Tuesday’s Euromillions jackpot is worth a guaranteed €15 million (approximately £12 million) so if you fancy being the first Euromillions jackpot winner this month, buy your tickets online or from an authorized retailer and if luck is on your side you could start this month with a huge eight-figure jackpot win. Good luck and enjoy the game!
