Support and Resistance Levels for Large-Cap Stocks

Here we report our computations of stock support and resistance levels. Support levels are where prices have bounced back up in the past after falling and resistance levels where stocks retreated after going higher. When new information is sufficient to move stock prices above prior resistance levels, the old resistance becomes new support and resistance is listed as "--" for not applicable. Similarly, when old support levels are broken to the downside, old support becomes new resistance and support is listed as "--" for not applicable.
