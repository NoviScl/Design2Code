France Lands Sixth EuroMillions Jackpot Win of 2013

Published: Mon, 1 July 2013 - 12:27pm
Last Updated: Thu, 8 December 2016 - 1:54pm
Published By: Euro-Millions.com

A ticket purchased in France won Friday's Euromillions jackpot worth €15,000,000 / £12,843,000, making it the sixth French EuroMillions jackpot win of 2013. Along with the jackpot winner, Friday's draw saw 2,710,031 players also win prizes.
