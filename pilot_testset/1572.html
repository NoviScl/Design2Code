Skin Care Centre

Skin Care Centre boiler upgrade was done in 2011. This 600 MBH Aerco Condensing boiler replaced an atmospheric 1,000 MBH boiler which largely served the main air handling unit in the building.

Thanks to Lockerbie and Hole for the installation work, VGH for assistance, Murray Mechanical and Fortis for the boiler and generous incentive!

 

Child Care Centre (Kids in General)

The VGH Child Care Centre (Kids In General) condensing boiler upgrade was done in 2012. The new Viessmann 380 MBH boiler was installed to replace existing mid efficient legacy boiler and new controls and zone heating and ventilation strategy was employed.

Thanks to Chapman Burner and ESC Automation for the installation work and VGH for assistance and also Veismann for the equipment and Fortis.
