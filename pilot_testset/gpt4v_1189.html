Classic Fan Review Rochester, NY 2005

Wednesday, 10th July 2013

Def Leppard played a show on the Rock of Ages tour in Rochester, NY on 10th July 2005 and a fan review was sent in by Janeen.

The show took place at the Frontier Field. Three other fan reviews are available from this show.

Janeen - Fan Review Quote

"I won a soundcheck auction through Raven Drum, that was not only to include soundcheck, but to meet Rick Allen and have early entry to the stadium. Soundcheck didn't happen, but only because at this point they didn't really play/practice for it - so we were told, and after seeing them perform, that was dead on - of the tour they really didn't need any reason."
