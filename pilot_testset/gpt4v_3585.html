World Premiere of John Casken's Madonna of Silence

Feb. 01, 2019

On February 28

the Hallé Orchestra, led by Jamie Phillips, perform the world premiere of John Casken's Madonna of Silence at The Bridgewater Hall in Manchester.

"Madonna of Silence is far from the quiet piece implied by the title."

(John Casken and Katy Jones on 'The Madonna of Silence')
