Surgical and Cytology Specimens

Specimens submitted for cytological or pathological examination must be accompanied with a completed requisition form indicating the following:
  1. Patient's name and date of birth
  2. Date and time of specimen collection
  3. Specimen source
  4. Submitting physician's name
