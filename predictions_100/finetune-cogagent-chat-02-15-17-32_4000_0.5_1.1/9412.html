About Us

Mission Statement

Mason County Fire District is dedicated to the protection of life and property.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to serve and protect our community by educating, equipping, and training our membership. We strive to improve any emergency situation.

Values Statement

Our organizational values are a matter of PRIDE: Professionalism, We strive for excellence through appearance, reliability, and responsibility. We have a duty to our actions, Dedication. We are committed to earning and maintaining the respect and E dedication. We encourage each member to achieve his or her highest potential.
