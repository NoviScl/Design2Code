About Us

Mission Statement

Mason County Fire District #6 is dedicated to the protection of life and property.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to serve and protect our community by educating, equipping, and training our memberships with the knowledge and skills to save lives and protect property.

Values Statement

Our organizational values are a matter of PR.I.D.E. - Professionalism, We strive for excellence, Integrity, and Dependability. We strive to improve any emergency or situation encountered.
