"Skully" dining table
Kao wood, bronze
84"L x 45"W x 29"H

The legs were borrowed from the "Aldo" table but with an asymmetrical Kao wood top. The inlays on the table surface are bronze.

The kitchen where this table resides was voted the best kitchen in America in 2002 by the Kitchen and Bath Association. You can view the article here: San Francisco Chronicle.
