KAM-TITAN

The Modular Knee Arthrodesis System

Based on the design of the MRP-TITAN system, the KAM-TITAN knee arthrodesis system is available in anatomic left and right variants in addition to a neutral module.

  • Configuration example: KAM-TITAN neutral (0°)
  • Variable and Anatomic
  • Earliest Possible Mobility
  • Excellent Long-term Results
  • Can be used with extremely poor bone stock
VARIABLE AND ANATOMIC

Neural and anatomic left and right versions (6° valgus, 7° flexion) available

  • Configuration example: KAM-TITAN variable and anatomic (6° valgus, 7° flexion)
EARLIEST POSSIBLE MOBILITY

Cementless implantation or optional cemented stems

  • Configuration example: KAM-TITAN early (0°)
EXCELLENT LONG-TERM RESULTS

Best results in long-term studies with a high degree of patient satisfaction

  • Configuration example: KAM-TITAN excellent long-term results (0°)
CAN BE USED WITH EXTREMELY POOR BONE STOCK

Can be used without restriction where bony ingrowth is absent or unlikely

  • Configuration example: KAM-TITAN can be used with extremely poor bone stock (0°)
