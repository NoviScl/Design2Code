Characteristics

Generally, a Captain's Log video is one in which Chris addresses his fanbase, a sort of video blog where he asks his True and Loyal fanbase to be his personal army or to express his hatred about some troll .

Chris and Star Trek

Interestingly enough, Chris is not a Trekker/Trekkie and had never seen any Star Trek media as of 2009 .

Captain's Blog

November 2009 , Chris began maintaining an irregular text blog on the CWCipedia , featuring updates on the progress of his comic and his quest to turn Sonichu into the heart of a multimedia empire.

Goddess Log

letter from jail dated 8 November 2021 , Chris reveals that he is keeping a journal in jail called his "Goddess Log". He intends to release it to the public when he is released.
