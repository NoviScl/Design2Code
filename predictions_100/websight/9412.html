About Us

Mission Statement

Mason County Fire District #6 is our vision to protect our community by equipping, equipping, and training our members. We strive to provide high level of emergency services to our community.

Values Statement

Our organizational values are a matter of pride. Professionalism: We strive for excellence through accountability, reliability, and integrity. We will be honest and accountable for our actions.

Dedication: We encourage and encourage our members to strive for excellence. We encourage our members to take pride in their positions.

Inclusivity: We believe in the power of diversity and our partners in our community.
