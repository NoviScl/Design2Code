About Us

Mission Statement

Mason County Fire District is dedicated to the protection of life and property.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to serve and protect our community by educating, equipping, and training our membership. We strive to improve any emergency situation.

Values Statement

Our organizational values are a matter of PRIDE: Professionalism. We strive for excellence through appearance, reliability, and responsibility. We have a duty to our citizens, to maintain the respect and dignity we earn in our profession.

Integrity. We will be honest and accountable for our actions.

Dedication. We are committed to earning and maintaining the respect and trust of the public.

Education. We are committed to earning and maintaining the respect and education of our members.

Excellence. We strive to improve every day.

Respect. We treat all people with respect and dignity.

Community Service. We actively participate in community service events and programs.
